Casanare

Casanare, located in the eastern region of Colombia, is a department known for its rich "llanera" culture and its vast expanse of plains. Its capital, Yopal, is an important economic and administrative center. Casanare stands out for its oil and agricultural industry, with cattle ranching being a prominent traditional activity. Its "llanero" landscape, full of savannas and rivers, offers a unique natural environment and is home to diverse flora and fauna.
